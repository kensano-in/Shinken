Questions

You Asked. We Answer.

What exactly is ShinChat?
+
ShinChat is a next-generation messaging platform built for speed, privacy, and intelligence. Think the community power of Discord, the privacy of Signal, and AI built directly into your workflow — all under one roof. It's infrastructure for the next decade of communication.
When will it launch publicly?
+
Full public launch is targeted for Q3 2026. We're currently in limited beta. Sign up with your email above to get early access notifications and potentially jump the queue when invite slots open.
Is it really end-to-end encrypted?
+
Yes, every single message — text, files, voice, video — is encrypted end-to-end using AES-256. We operate on a zero-knowledge architecture. Our servers never have access to your decrypted messages. Not even us. That's the promise and it's built into the protocol.
Will there be a free tier?
+
Yes. ShinChat has a generous free tier for personal and small group use. Premium unlocks larger file transfers, advanced AI features, analytics, and priority infrastructure. Early access users get an extended premium trial at launch — another reason to sign up now.
Who is behind ShinChat?
+
ShinChat is a Shinken product — an independent software studio. We're a small, focused team obsessed with building tools that are powerful and beautiful. No VC pressure, no compromise on privacy, no dark patterns. Just people who care deeply about getting this right.
How is this different from Discord or Telegram?
+
Discord is great for communities but not private. Telegram is private but limited for large communities. ShinChat does both — true E2E encryption at scale, rich community features, deep AI integration, and a developer-first API. We're not competing — we're building the next layer.
Will there be a self-hosted option?
+
Yes, self-hosting is on the 2027 roadmap. Enterprise teams and privacy-conscious organizations will be able to run ShinChat on their own infrastructure while keeping the full feature set. An open-source SDK is also planned for community contributions.
